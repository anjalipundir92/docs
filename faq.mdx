## Getting Started

<Accordion title="How many properties can I manage?">
  The number of properties you can manage depends on your Viithii subscription plan:

  | Plan | Properties |
  |---|---|
  | **Starter** | 1 property |
  | **Growth** | Up to 10 properties |
  | **Enterprise** | Unlimited properties |

  You can upgrade your plan at any time from **Settings** → **Billing** → **Upgrade Plan** without losing any existing data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
  Yes. Viithii offers a **14-day free trial** on all plans with full access to every feature — no credit card required. At the end of your trial, you can choose a paid plan to continue. If you don't subscribe, your account is paused and your data is retained for 30 days before deletion.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I import data from another property management tool?">
  Yes. Viithii supports **CSV import** for properties and tenants, making it straightforward to migrate from spreadsheets or another platform. To import, go to **Settings** → **Import Data** and download the CSV template for properties or tenants. Fill in the template with your existing data and upload the file. The importer validates each row and highlights any errors before committing the data.

  If you need help formatting a complex export from another tool, contact Viithii support and our onboarding team can assist.
</Accordion>

## Payments

<Accordion title="When does rent money land in my bank account?">
  After a tenant completes an ACH (bank transfer) payment, funds are deposited to your verified bank account within **2–3 business days**. The exact timing depends on your bank's processing schedule. Payouts do not process on weekends or federal holidays — payments made on a Friday may not arrive until the following Wednesday.

  You can track the status of every payout in **Financials** → **Payouts**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods can tenants use?">
  Tenants can pay rent through the following methods via the Viithii tenant portal:

  - **Bank transfer (ACH)** — connect a checking or savings account directly
  - **Credit card** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  - **Debit card** — any card with a Visa or Mastercard logo

  You can restrict available payment methods from **Settings** → **Payments** if you prefer tenants to pay by ACH only.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there transaction fees?">
  Transaction fees vary by payment method:

  | Method | Fee |
  |---|---|
  | **ACH / Bank Transfer** | Free |
  | **Credit or Debit Card** | 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction |

  Card processing fees are passed to the landlord by default. You can optionally pass card fees to tenants from **Settings** → **Payments** → **Fee Policy**.
</Accordion>

## Leases

<Accordion title="Can tenants sign leases digitally?">
  Yes. Viithii includes built-in e-signature at no extra cost on all plans. When you create a lease and click **Send for Signature**, Viithii emails all signing parties a secure link. Signers can review and sign the lease from any device — no account or app download required. Once all parties sign, everyone receives a copy of the executed lease by email, and it's stored permanently in Viithii.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens when a lease expires?">
  Viithii sends you a reminder notification before the lease expiry date (the timing depends on your [notification settings](/configuration/notifications)). When the lease reaches its end date, you have three options:

  - **Renew the lease** — create a new fixed-term lease with updated terms and send it for e-signature
  - **Switch to month-to-month** — convert the lease to a month-to-month agreement directly from the lease record
  - **Terminate the tenancy** — begin the move-out process from the tenant's profile

  Until you take action, the lease status changes to **Expired** and the tenant remains in your system as active.
</Accordion>

## Tenants

<Accordion title="Can tenants pay rent without using the portal?">
  Yes. If a tenant pays by cash, check, or any method outside Viithii, you can record the payment manually on their behalf. Go to **Tenants** → select the tenant → **Record Payment**, enter the amount, payment date, and method, and save. The payment appears in the tenant's ledger and in your financial records exactly like a portal payment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does the tenant portal work?">
  Every tenant you add to Viithii receives a free tenant portal account — there's no additional cost or setup required on your end. From the portal, tenants can:

  - **Pay rent** online using their preferred payment method
  - **Submit maintenance requests** with photos and descriptions
  - **View and download** their current lease and payment history
  - **Receive notifications** for upcoming rent due dates, payment confirmations, and maintenance status updates

  Tenants receive an email invitation to set up their portal account when you add them to a lease. You can resend the invitation at any time from their tenant profile.
</Accordion>

## Account & Billing

<Accordion title="How do I upgrade my plan?">
  To upgrade, go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Upgrade Plan**. Select the plan you want, review the pricing, and enter your payment details. Upgrades take effect immediately — you gain access to the higher plan's features right away, and your billing is prorated for the remainder of the current period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
  Yes, you can cancel your Viithii subscription at any time:

  - **Monthly plans** — cancellation takes effect immediately. Your access ends at the close of the current billing cycle and you are not charged again.
  - **Annual plans** — annual subscriptions are non-refundable, but your access continues through the end of the paid period. You will not be auto-renewed after cancellation.

  To cancel, go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Cancel Subscription** and follow the prompts. Your data is retained for 30 days after the subscription ends before permanent deletion.
</Accordion>
