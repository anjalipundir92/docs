Before you can add properties or invite tenants, you need a fully configured Viithii account. This page walks you through the sign-up process, email verification, profile completion, and plan selection — and explains how Individual Landlords and Property Management Companies should configure their workspaces differently.

## Create and Activate Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up at app.viithiisys.com">
    Open [app.viithiisys.com](https://app.viithiisys.com) in your browser and click **Get Started Free**. Enter your first name, last name, email address, and a password that is at least 10 characters long and includes a number and a special character.

    Click **Create Account** to submit the form. Viithii will immediately send a verification email to the address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email Address">
    Open the verification email from **no-reply@viithiisys.com** and click the **Confirm Email** button. This link is valid for 24 hours.

    Once confirmed, you will be redirected back to the platform and logged in automatically. If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or click **Resend Verification Email** on the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    On first login, Viithii prompts you to finish your profile. Fill in the following fields:

    - **Display Name** — the name tenants and vendors will see in communications
    - **Phone Number** — used for SMS notifications and two-factor authentication
    - **Company / Business Name** — appears on leases, invoices, and receipts (leave blank if you manage properties as an individual)
    - **Time Zone** — ensures due dates, reminders, and reports reflect your local time

    Click **Save Profile** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Subscription Plan">
    Viithii offers three plans based on portfolio size:

    | Plan | Units Included | Best For |
    |---|---|---|
    | **Starter** | Up to 5 units | Individual landlords with a small portfolio |
    | **Growth** | Up to 50 units | Independent landlords scaling their business |
    | **Pro** | Unlimited units | Property management companies |

    Select the plan that matches your current portfolio. You can upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing** without losing any data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Property">
    Once your plan is active, Viithii redirects you to the **Properties** screen. Click **Add Property**, enter the address and unit details, and save. This creates your first property record and unlocks the full platform workflow.

    For a detailed guide, see [Add a Property](/guides/add-property).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage Your Account Settings

After initial setup, you can update your account details at any time under **Settings** in the left sidebar.

<Accordion title="Profile Settings">
  Update your display name, phone number, company name, profile photo, and time zone. Changes take effect immediately and are reflected across all tenant-facing communications.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Password and Security">
  Change your password under **Settings → Security**. Choose a password you haven't used before, then click **Update Password**. You'll receive a confirmation email once the change is saved.

  Viithii also lets you review active sessions and revoke access from unrecognized devices on the same screen.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Billing and Subscription">
  View your current plan, upcoming renewal date, and invoice history under **Settings → Billing**. Add or update your payment method, upgrade your plan, or download PDF receipts for your records.

  If you need to cancel, click **Manage Subscription** and follow the prompts. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period.
</Accordion>

## Workspace Configuration by Account Type

The way you configure Viithii depends on whether you manage properties personally or as part of a professional management company.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Individual Landlord">
    If you own and manage your own rental properties, configure your workspace as follows:

    - **Business Name** — leave blank or enter your own name; invoices will display your personal name
    - **Team Members** — not required unless you have a business partner or assistant; add them under **Settings → Team**
    - **Branding** — optional; you can upload a logo to personalize tenant-facing emails on the Growth plan and above
    - **Bank Account** — connect a personal or business checking account under **Settings → Payments** to enable rent collection

    Individual landlords typically manage everything themselves, so default notification settings (email for all events) are a good starting point.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Property Management Company">
    If you manage properties on behalf of multiple property owners, set up your workspace as a company:

    - **Company Name** — enter your legal business name; this appears on all leases, notices, and payment receipts
    - **Team Members** — invite your staff under **Settings → Team** and assign roles (Admin, Manager, or Maintenance Coordinator) to control access levels
    - **Owner Portals** — enable owner reporting under **Settings → Owners** so property owners can log in and view financial statements for their units
    - **Branding** — upload your company logo and set a custom accent color to match your brand identity on all tenant communications
    - **Bank Account** — connect your business operating account and, if applicable, a separate trust account for holding security deposits

    On the Pro plan, you can create separate property groups for each owner client, keeping financials and reports partitioned by portfolio.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Viithii supports two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts. To enable it, go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication** and follow the prompts to link an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. Enabling 2FA is strongly recommended, especially if your account is connected to a live bank account for rent collection.
</Note>
