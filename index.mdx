## How Viithii Works

New to the platform? Here's the typical flow every landlord or property manager follows when they get started with Viithii.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account and Workspace">
    Sign up at [app.viithiisys.com](https://app.viithiisys.com), verify your email, and complete your profile. Choose the subscription plan that fits your portfolio size — you can upgrade at any time as you grow. See [Account Setup](/account-setup) for a full walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Properties and Configure Leases">
    Add your rental properties and individual units to the platform. Set rent amounts, upload photos, and create lease templates so you're ready to onboard tenants as soon as a unit becomes available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Tenants and Start Collecting Rent">
    Send tenants a digital invite to complete their profiles and sign their leases online. Connect your bank account, configure your rent collection settings, and let Viithii handle payment reminders, receipts, and late fees automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
