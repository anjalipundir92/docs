Viithii is a modern, cloud-based property management platform built for landlords and property managers who want to spend less time on admin and more time growing their portfolio. Whether you manage a single unit or hundreds of properties, Viithii gives you a single workspace to handle every stage of the rental lifecycle — from listing and leasing to maintenance and monthly payments.

## What You Can Do with Viithii

Use the cards below to explore the core areas of the platform. Each section links to a dedicated concept page where you can learn how Viithii models that part of your business.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Properties" icon="building" href="/concepts/properties">
    Add and manage your rental units, set property details, upload photos, and track occupancy across your entire portfolio.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tenants" icon="users" href="/concepts/tenants">
    Maintain a complete record for every tenant — contact information, lease history, documents, and communication logs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leases" icon="file-contract" href="/concepts/leases">
    Create, sign, and store leases digitally. Automate renewals, track key dates, and keep all agreements in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments" icon="money-bill" href="/concepts/payments">
    Collect rent online, send automated reminders, record payments, and reconcile your ledger without manual entry.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Maintenance" icon="wrench" href="/guides/maintenance-requests">
    Let tenants submit maintenance requests, assign work orders to vendors, track progress, and log completed repairs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reports" icon="chart-bar" href="/guides/reports">
    Generate income statements, occupancy reports, and payment histories to keep your finances clear and audit-ready.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
