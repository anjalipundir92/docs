Viithii's reporting engine gives you a clear financial picture of your entire portfolio at any point in time. You can generate on-demand snapshots, export data in PDF or CSV format for your accountant, or schedule reports to arrive in your inbox automatically each month — all without leaving the platform.

## Available Reports

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Rent Roll" icon="table-list" href="/guides/reports">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Income Statement" icon="chart-line" href="/guides/reports">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Occupancy Report" icon="building" href="/guides/reports">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Maintenance Cost Report" icon="wrench" href="/guides/reports">

</CardGroup>

## Generating a Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reports">
    Click **Reports** in the left sidebar to open the reporting dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a report type">
    Choose from the report types listed — **Rent Roll**, **Income Statement**, **Occupancy Report**, or **Maintenance Cost Report**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a property">
    Use the **Property** dropdown to select a specific property or choose **All Properties** to aggregate data across your entire portfolio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date range">
    Enter a **Start Date** and **End Date** for the reporting period. For rent roll reports, Viithii defaults to the current date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and download">
    Click **Generate**. Viithii processes the report and displays a preview on screen. Click **Download PDF** or **Download CSV** to save a copy to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When preparing for tax season, export your Income Statement and Maintenance Cost Report as CSV files and send them directly to your accountant or import them into your accounting software. The CSV format is compatible with QuickBooks, Xero, and most other bookkeeping tools.
</Tip>

## Scheduled Reports

Instead of generating reports manually each month, you can configure Viithii to deliver them automatically to your email inbox on a recurring schedule.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Scheduled Reports">
    Go to **Reports** → **Scheduled Reports** and click **New Schedule**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the schedule">
    Select the report type, the property scope (specific property or all), and set the frequency to **Monthly**. Choose the day of the month you want the report delivered — for example, the 1st or 5th.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add recipients">
    Enter one or more email addresses in the **Recipients** field. You can include co-owners, your accountant, or any team member who needs the data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the export format">
    Select **PDF**, **CSV**, or **Both** as the attachment format for the scheduled delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the schedule">
    Click **Save Schedule**. Viithii will send the report automatically on the configured day each month until you pause or delete the schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Viithii retains all financial records for **7 years** in compliance with standard accounting and audit requirements. You can access and export historical reports for any period within that window at any time from the **Reports** dashboard.
</Note>
