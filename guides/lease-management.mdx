Viithii handles the entire lease lifecycle from a single dashboard — drafting agreements, collecting e-signatures, sending renewal notices, and processing move-outs. Keeping every lease digital means no lost paperwork, instant access to executed copies, and a clear audit trail for every change made along the way.

## Creating a New Lease

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the New Lease form">
    Navigate to **Leases** in the left sidebar and click **New Lease** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the tenant and unit">
    Search for an existing tenant by name or email, or invite a new tenant by entering their contact details. Then choose the unit they will occupy from the **Unit** dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the lease type and dates">
    Choose either **Fixed-Term** or **Month-to-Month** as the lease type. Enter the **Start Date** and, for fixed-term leases, the **End Date**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter rent and deposit details">
    Fill in the **Monthly Rent** amount, the **Security Deposit** amount, and the **Rent Due Day** (for example, the 1st or 15th of each month).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the late fee policy">
    Toggle on **Late Fees** and specify the grace period (in days), the fee type (flat amount or percentage), and the fee value. Viithii applies this automatically when rent is overdue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add custom clauses">
    Scroll to the **Additional Clauses** section and type in any property-specific terms — pet policies, parking rules, utility responsibilities, or anything else not covered by the standard template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send for e-signature">
    Review the full lease preview, then click **Send for Signature**. Both you and the tenant receive an email with a secure signing link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## E-Signatures

When you send a lease for e-signature, Viithii emails a secure signing link to all parties listed on the agreement. Each party opens the link, reviews the full document, and clicks to sign digitally — no downloads or printer required.

Once every party has signed, Viithii automatically marks the lease as **Executed** and stores the countersigned PDF in the lease record. You and the tenant can download the executed copy at any time from **Leases** → select the lease → **Documents**.

<Note>
  E-signatures collected through Viithii are legally binding in most jurisdictions under applicable electronic signature laws. Check with your local legal counsel if you have questions about enforceability in your area.
</Note>

## Renewing a Lease

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the expiring lease">
    Go to **Leases** and filter by **Expiring Soon** to surface leases approaching their end date. Click the lease you want to renew.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a renewal">
    Click **Renew Lease**. Viithii pre-fills a new lease draft using the existing terms. Adjust the new end date, update the rent amount if needed, and modify any clauses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the renewal for signature">
    Click **Send for Signature** to deliver the renewal agreement to the tenant. Once both parties sign, the renewed lease becomes active and the previous one is archived automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Set up **Renewal Reminders** under **Settings → Notifications** to receive email alerts at 90, 60, and 30 days before a lease expires. This gives you and your tenant plenty of time to decide on renewal terms without last-minute pressure.
</Tip>

## Terminating a Lease

<Warning>
  Lease termination is irreversible. Once you confirm termination in Viithii, the lease status changes permanently and cannot be undone. Reconcile and return (or withhold) the security deposit **before** finalizing termination to ensure all financial records are accurate.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the lease record">
    Go to **Leases** and select the active lease you need to terminate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate termination">
    Click **Terminate Lease** and select the reason (tenant-initiated, manager-initiated, lease violation, etc.) along with the **Move-Out Date**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the move-out checklist">
    Work through the **Move-Out Checklist** to document the unit's condition, record any damages, and calculate any deductions from the security deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconcile the security deposit">
    Navigate to **Deposits** within the lease record. Apply any approved deductions, then process the deposit refund to the tenant. Viithii logs the full itemization for your records.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm termination">
    Click **Confirm Termination**. The unit status updates to **Vacant** and the lease moves to the **Closed** archive.
  </Step>
</Steps>
